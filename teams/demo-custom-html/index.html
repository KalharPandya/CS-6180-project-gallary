Generative Adversarial Network Art

Henry Zhao & Iris Patel — CS-6180

Abstract

We trained a StyleGAN2-ADA model on a curated dataset of 12,000 museum artwork images spanning Impressionism, Cubism, and Abstract Expressionism. The resulting model generates novel artworks that blind human evaluators rated as indistinguishable from real works 38% of the time.

Human Evaluation

We ran a two-alternative forced choice study with 120 participants via Prolific. Participants were shown pairs of real and generated artworks and asked to identify the real one. Accuracy was 62% (chance = 50%), confirming high visual fidelity.
